# Product

## Register

product

## Users

PKI administrators, security engineers, platform teams, auditors, application owners, certificate requesters, approvers, and constrained autonomous agents operating in large or regulated organisations. They work primarily on desktop displays and need to make high-consequence lifecycle decisions quickly without losing evidence, ownership, or tenant context.

## Product Purpose

BlakCert is the operational control plane for discovering, governing, issuing, deploying, renewing, revoking, and auditing digital certificates. Success means every certificate has an accountable owner, explainable risk, policy state, deployment history, and safe automation path through the same authorised service layer used by humans, REST clients, and MCP agents.

## Brand Personality

Precise, assured, restrained. The product should feel trustworthy under pressure and candid about risk, uncertainty, approvals, and partial failure.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative security theatre, neon hacker styling, oversized metric cards, excessive gradients, glass surfaces, sparse consumer layouts, hidden table actions, and dashboards that summarise risk without linking to operational work.

## Design Principles

1. Evidence before assertion: explain every risk, policy result, and agent recommendation.
2. Dense, not cramped: optimise for expert scanning while preserving clear hierarchy and accessible targets.
3. Consequences stay visible: show scope, environment, reversibility, approval, and audit impact before action.
4. One operational language: humans, APIs, jobs, and agents use the same domain contracts and status vocabulary.
5. Safe defaults are legible: dry-run, least privilege, and separation of duties should be obvious in the interface.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.2 AA. All workflows support keyboard operation, visible focus, semantic headings and tables, accessible names, non-colour status cues, reduced motion, high contrast, and concise error summaries.
